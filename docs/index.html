MSc in Experiential Digital Marketing Communications (XDMarComs)

Master the Future of Marketing

Purpose:
To empower ambitious students and professionals to master digital storytelling, navigate the data-driven marketing landscape, and lead brands in delivering meaningful, memorable experiences to the connected consumer.

Mission:
To foster a student-centric, innovation-driven learning environment where individuals thrive academically, professionally, and personally.

We empower our students with:

  • Advanced skills in AI, data analytics, IoT, and phygital marketing
  • Essential soft skills such as adaptability, agility, resilience, and collaboration
  • The capacity to create sustainable value in an ever-evolving digital landscape

What Sets Our Programme Apart

Flexible Delivery Structure

XDMarComs offers a flexible, hybrid learning programme with intensive five-week modules. Combining weekend classes, online seminars, and self-paced content, it supports professionals in balancing work with academic growth.

Integrated Cross-Disciplinary Framework

Excellence in modern marketing demands a multidisciplinary approach. Our curriculum integrates technology, psychology, analytics, design, consumer culture, and communication to equip graduates with strategic, cross-functional solutions.

Continuous Professional Development

Beyond career advancement, the programme cultivates self-directed learning and metacognitive skills, empowering graduates to adapt and grow continuously in evolving digital marketing landscapes.
